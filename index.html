Gentoo

The GIF at the Bottom of the page will stretch for miles on and on when on a mobile device. I will fix this later. It is normal on 1080p 16:9 screens.

Gentoo is a "build it yourself" distribution which is extremely customizable where you are able to know every single nook and cranny of your operating system. It's an extremely long compile and make time if you do not use .bin packages where it can span over 10 hours just to get to a TTY. It is certainly not recommended for beginners or people who have actually better things to do in their life then sit for 2+ hours just to get a zen kernel.
